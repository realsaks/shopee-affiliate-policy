Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Shopee Affiliate Auto Post handles information.

2. Information We Access

  • Social media account credentials (stored locally, never shared)
  • Public product information from Shopee affiliate feeds
  • Post performance metrics from connected platforms

3. How We Use Information

  • To post affiliate content to your social media accounts
  • To track post performance for optimization
  • All data is stored locally and not shared with third parties

4. Data Storage

All credentials and data are stored locally on your own server. No data is transmitted to external servers operated by this App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App connects to TikTok (TikTok for Developers API), Facebook (Meta Graph API), and Shopee Affiliate Program. Each platform governs their own data practices.
