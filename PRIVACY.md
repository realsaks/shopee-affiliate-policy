# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 31, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Shopee Affiliate Auto Post ("the App") handles information.

## 2. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following to function:
- Social media account credentials (stored locally, never shared)
- Public product information from Shopee affiliate feeds
- Post performance metrics from connected platforms

## 3. How We Use Information

- To post affiliate content to your social media accounts
- To track post performance for optimization
- All data is stored locally and is not shared with third parties

## 4. Data Storage

All credentials and data are stored locally on your own server/device. No data is transmitted to external servers operated by this App.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App connects to:
- TikTok (via TikTok for Developers API)
- Facebook (via Meta Graph API)
- Shopee Affiliate Program

Each platform has its own privacy policy which governs their data practices.
